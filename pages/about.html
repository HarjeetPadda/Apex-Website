About Us.

Apex Transport and Distribution is a Transport and Warehousing company that operates over 200 pieces of equipment including Prime movers, Trailing equipment and Dollies Nationwide. We have been operating since 2016 and have seen steady growth over the years in Transport and continue to remain ambitious about our journey ahead, even in this everchanging business landscape. We specialize in Interstate Transport of General Palletized Freight, Specialized freight, Hot shots, Car Transport and Warehouse and Distribution. We currently have Depots in Melbourne, Adelaide and Perth and have seen tremendous growth in our business due to doing things the right way.
