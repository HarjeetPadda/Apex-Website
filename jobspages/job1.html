Road Train Drivers
Adelaide & Perth

Above Award Wages

Full Time

Due to significant growth, Apex Transport & Distribution is seeking experienced Road Train Drivers to work on interstate runs.

We currently have roles available for changeover Drivers based in Adelaide & Perth departing every Tuesday, Thursday & Saturday.
We offer above award wages and a safe work environment.
Apex Transport operates a modern fleet, multiple depot locations to facilitate our Drivers with better working conditions.
Full time and casual roles are available for the the right candidates who:

- have a strong commitment to health and safety.
- is customer service focused.
- Basic Fatigue Management Trained.
- Minimum 2 year's experience with Multi Combination.
- have a safe driving record.
- DG Licence preferred.

While working with Apex Transport you will follow fatigue management guidelines and will adhere to all OH&S, legislative, and customer site obligations.
You will be reporting to the Operations Manager, perform your allocated task within Apex Transport safety and compliance standards and values.

You will need to:

- provide a copy of your current driving licence and driving history.
- undergo medical examination, including drug and alcohol screening. - be inducted into Apex Transport systems and sites.

To Apply

Please submit your application including Cover Letter, CV and Referees.

admin@apextransport.com.au

Your application will include the following questions:
1. Which of the following statements best describes your right to work in Australia?
2. Do you have a heavy vehicle driver's licence?
3. Are you willing to provide a copy of your driving record history?
4. How much notice are you required to give your current employer?
5. Are you willing to undergo pre-employment drug and alcohol screening?
